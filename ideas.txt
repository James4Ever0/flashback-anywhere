ideas on mode change:

- always on transfer, abolish deep sleep, use light sleep or none, can transfer photo and audio as long as the device is on with bluetooth, wifi or serial
- use another special chip to perform power relay and sd card switching, switch from sd card reader or just forward sd to the cam board and power the cam board on
- check if board has vbus pinout and if so design a special pcb wiring the vbus to another gpio with two resistors. vbus -> r1 -> [gpio_x, r2] -> gnd
- wire en with ground and a button to perform reset
- use a physical toggle switch on battery
- set baud rate to a fixed rate between text mode and transfer mode, do not switch baud rate.
